Our Story

Built by Gamers,

Alvin's Games & Electronics was founded with a simple mission: to bring world-class gaming products to Zambia at honest prices with exceptional service.

What started as a passion project has grown into one of the most trusted gaming stores in Lusaka. We stock only genuine products — from the latest PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X to Nintendo Switch, video games, peripherals, and consumer electronics.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a competitive esports player, we have everything you need, and we're here to help you every step of the way.
